Our research with **CIENFI** and the **Secretaría Distrital de Desarrollo Económico de Bogotá** was featured in **El Tiempo**. We estimate Bogotá's GDP at 450x450m grid resolution using satellite nighttime radiance, urban mobility data, and census data — with less than 1 percentage point error. [See more](https://www.eltiempo.com/bogota/satelites-para-monitorear-la-economia-en-bogota-asi-se-utilizan-las-imagenes-desde-el-espacio-para-ver-el-comportamiento-del-pib-y-el-empleo-3511019).
